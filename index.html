FAQ

Who are these webinars for? 

These webinars are designed for content creators, aspiring influencers, social media professionals, and anyone interested in building or supporting a career within the creator economy. They are open to all creators at different stages of their journey.

Do I have to be a REACH member or Shine Talent creator to RSVP and attend?

No, you don’t need to be a REACH member or a signed Shine Talent Group creator to RSVP or attend. These webinars are open to all interested participants.

Do I have to RSVP for every single webinar?

No, once you RSVP, you’ll be kept in the loop for all upcoming webinars, so there’s no need to RSVP individually for each session. As a reminder, these webinars are COMPLETELY free for content creators.

Do I have to attend every webinar?

No, you are welcome to attend as many or as few webinars as you like. Each session is standalone but part of the overall curriculum.

How long is every webinar?

Each webinar is 50 minutes long, followed by 30 minutes of content review and 20 minutes for Q&A, making the total session time about 1 hour and 40 minutes.

Will the webinars be recorded?

Yes, the webinars will be recorded, and a post-webinar recap will be sent to your email. However, it is currently undetermined if the full recording will be provided to attendees, so we highly encourage you to attend live to maximize the experience.
